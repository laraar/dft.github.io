About Deerfield Theater for Young Audiences

Deerfield Theater for Young Audiences (DTYA) was created in 2022 with the intent to introduce theatrical experiences to audience members of all ages, and with its inaugural production, Fancy Nancy: The Musical, taking the stage the following year.
